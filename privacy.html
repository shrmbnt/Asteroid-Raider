Privacy Policy

Asteroid Raider

Asteroid Raider is designed to collect only the data needed for gameplay, leaderboards, and anonymous gameplay analytics.

What We Store

  • Nickname: a nickname you choose at first launch (2-18 characters). It is stored locally on your device and submitted with your score to our leaderboard database in Google Firebase Firestore.
  • Anonymous device identifier: a UUID generated on your device. It is used only to prevent duplicate leaderboard entries from the same device.
  • Anonymous gameplay events: Google Analytics 4 may receive gameplay metrics such as run duration, score, coins earned, ship choice, and session length. No real-world contact information is attached to these events by Asteroid Raider.

What We Do Not Collect

  • Email addresses, phone numbers, or real names
  • Location
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Contacts
  • Photos, microphone, or camera data

Third Parties

  • Google Firebase Firestore: leaderboard storage
  • Google Analytics: aggregated gameplay metrics

Children

Asteroid Raider is rated 9+. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data that should be removed, contact us using the address below.

Data Deletion

To request deletion of your leaderboard entry, contact goldstardevapp@gmail.com. Include the nickname shown in the game so we can locate the entry.
